Best Places to Eat & Drink
in the City

Maison Premiere

A Williamsburg oyster & cocktail bar that serves small plates in a New Orleans-styled setting with an atmospheric garden.

Rosemary's

A West Village airy neighborhood Italian restaurant with a rooftop farm for seasonal Italian dishes, house-made pastas & notable wines.

Sushi by Bou

Quick-serve, 10-seat omakase sushi counter in Jue Lan Club offering a single 12-piece menu.

Cafe Colette

Colette embraces the idea of New American cuisine, and is influenced by flavors and techniques from Spain, Italy, Central America, and beyond.

Baby's All Right

A bar in historic Williamsburg with live music, gourmet bar fare, creative drinks & weekend brunch.

Pokito

Cozy, eclectic, artist-run neighborhood bar. serving seasonal latin + asian inspired cocktails. in the heart of south Williamsburg.

Skinny Dennis

Down-home, honky-tonk saloon pairing a woody space with 18 different beers on tap, games & live country music.
